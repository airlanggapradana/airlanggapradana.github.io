About

Hello! My name is Airlangga Pradana and I am a freelance 3D artist with over 5 years of experience in the industry. I specialize in creating high-quality 3D models, texturing, lighting, and rendering for a variety of projects, including TV commercials, product visualization, and physics simulations.

In addition to my technical skills, I am also a strong communicator and team player. I am able to work independently or as part of a larger team, and I am comfortable taking on projects of varying scope and complexity.I am excited to bring my skills and experience to your project as a freelance 3D artist and I am confident that I can help bring your vision to life. Thank you for considering me for this opportunity.
